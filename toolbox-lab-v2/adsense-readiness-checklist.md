# AdSense Readiness Checklist

## Content

- [ ] The homepage has a real usable tool above or near the first viewport.
- [ ] The tool has original explanatory content below it.
- [ ] Calculation assumptions or data sources are clearly stated.
- [ ] FAQ answers are specific to the tool, not generic filler.
- [ ] Pages are not copied from templates without meaningful editing.
- [ ] Thin duplicate pages are avoided.

## Trust And Policy

- [ ] About page explains who operates the site and why.
- [ ] Contact page includes a reachable email or form.
- [ ] Privacy Policy explains cookies, ads, analytics, and user choices.
- [ ] Terms page explains reference-only use and prohibited behavior.
- [ ] Editorial Policy explains content and correction standards.
- [ ] Disclaimer is included when the tool touches money, contracts, health, legal, tax, or employment decisions.

## Technical

- [ ] Every page has a unique title and meta description.
- [ ] Canonical URLs are set to production URLs before deployment.
- [ ] `robots.txt` allows crawling.
- [ ] `sitemap.xml` lists public pages with production URLs.
- [ ] Mobile layout has no clipped text, overlapping controls, or horizontal scroll.
- [ ] Forms and buttons have accessible labels.
- [ ] The site loads without console errors.
- [ ] No broken internal links.

## Ad Placement Later

- [ ] Ads do not push the primary tool below usability.
- [ ] Ads are visually distinct from tool results and navigation.
- [ ] No accidental clicks are encouraged.
- [ ] No placeholder ad boxes are submitted before approval unless needed for layout testing and clearly hidden from production.
