# Identity Brief

## Working Name

Undecided.

## Audience

People who need a quick practical calculation, comparison, or planning aid and want the assumptions explained clearly.

## Site Promise

Fast tools with plain-language explanations, transparent assumptions, and no unnecessary account requirement.

## Tone

Clear, calm, practical, and specific. Avoid hype, vague productivity claims, and thin auto-generated wording.

## Trust Signals

- Visible calculation assumptions
- Update date for important guides
- Contact path for corrections
- Privacy and cookie explanation
- Editorial policy for tool logic and content review
- Clear disclaimer when results are only estimates

## Open Decisions

- Final site name
- Primary tool category
- Whether V2 should be Korean-only or bilingual
- Whether to keep the same brand family as V1 or test a different identity
